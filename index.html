How to Use Napper

What is Napper?

Napper generates all possible sleep schedules within your constraints and scores them based on sleep quality targets.

Understanding Constraints

  • Wake Time: You can wake up between 6:30 AM and 8:30 AM
  • Nap Duration: Naps last 1, 1.5, or 2 hours
  • Awake Duration: Awake periods last 1 or 1.5 hours
  • Bedtime: Schedule must end by 8:30 PM
  • Precision: All times use 30-minute increments

Using Filters

Filters help narrow down schedules to your preferences:

Activity Choose "Nap", "Awake", or "NightTime"
Action Choose "Starts" or "Ends"
Time Select the specific time

Example Filters

  • Nap / Starts / 08:30 → Find schedules with a nap starting at 8:30 AM
  • Nap / Ends / 10:00 → Find schedules with a nap ending at 10:00 AM
  • NightTime / Ends / 06:30 → Wake up at 6:30 AM

Reading the Results

  • Score: Higher is better (based on matching sleep targets)
  • Nap Count: Number of naps in the schedule
  • Hours Columns: Breakdown of sleep distribution
  • Color Coding: Purple = Nap, Green = Awake, Blue = Night

Tips

  • Start with no filters to see all possibilities
  • Add filters to narrow down to schedules you like
  • Multiple filters narrow results further
  • Use the Apply button to update results
